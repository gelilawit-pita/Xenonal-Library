How to Borrow Books

Simple steps to check out materials from our library

1

Get a Library Card

Register for a free library card at any of our branches with valid ID and proof of address.

2

Find Your Books

Use our catalog to search for items and note their location and availability.

3

Check Out

Bring items to the circulation desk or use our self-checkout stations with your library card.

4

Enjoy & Return

Most items check out for 3 weeks. Return by the due date or renew online if needed.
